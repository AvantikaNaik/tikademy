About the Creator!

I'm Avantika Naik, recent graudate from Mansfield High School.

As part of the National Honor Society, I saw firsthand how many students needed private tutors but couldn't get them due to NHS rules.

As a tutor on my own time, I experienced how difficult it was to find a student, and my classmates and I discussed how we wished we could find students easier!

That's why I created ConnectEDU, a web-based application that connects students and tutors!
